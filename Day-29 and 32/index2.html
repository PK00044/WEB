Almora

Widely known for its picturesque places and cultural diversity, Almora is the cultural heart of the Kumaon region in Uttarakhand. The months April, May, June, September, October and November are highly favourable to visit this place. It is the perfect destination for the people having interest in history. The words of Mahatama Gandhi hold a mirror to the beauty of Almora, as he said, “In these hills, nature’s hospitality eclipses all men can ever do.
